Self Mastery

Learnings from 'Search Inside Yourself' by Chade-Meng Tan

20th August 2017

When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

I think sometimes I am just not on a good place to begin learning. I maybe have other factors like work/life stresses that mean I may have a disproportinate emotional response to programming.

I think some of my triggers may be that I am tired or have already been working too long on a problem. A trigger can be when I feel certain I have the correct code/solution but continue to get error messages. Or when I am not able to decipher what the error message is, or search on google and am not able to find the information I need. It is really satisfing though when you've been working on code/ a problem for a long time and then finally find the solution its a mixture of relief and joy and sometimes a little regret that you didn't find it earlier.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I really enjoyed the Siberian North exercise, it was really beneficial to slow things down and give yourself time to think about how to respond before having a quick impulsive reaction.
